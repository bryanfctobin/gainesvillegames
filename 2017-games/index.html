ABOUT

Gainesville, at its core, is a small town. We take care of each other, and we pride ourselves on making Gainesville a happy, healthy place to live. To help achieve this goal we have created the Gainesville Games, an annual field day fundraiser to help make sure the next generation is better than the last.

This year, we're proud to be working with the March of Dimes and their mission to end birth defects, premature birth and infant mortality. For almost 80 years, the March of Dimes has worked tirelessly to give every baby a fighting chance, and we can't think of a more worthy cause. Join us on September 24th to help give the next generation a better tomorrow.
